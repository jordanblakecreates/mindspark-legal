1. Introduction

MindSpark ("we", "our", "us") is a content creation and publishing application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. Our app is used solely for publishing video content to social media platforms.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app interacts with third-party APIs (such as TikTok and YouTube) solely for the purpose of publishing content. We do not access, collect, or store any user data from these platforms beyond what is necessary for authentication and content publishing.

4. Data Storage

We do not maintain any databases of user information. Authentication tokens are stored locally on the developer's device and are not shared with any third parties.

5. Cookies

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
